DOWNLOAD TIPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------

- On Sketchfab: click the model, then "Download 3D Model" button.
  Free models with CC licenses allow OBJ/GLB/GLTF download.

- On Thingiverse: click "Download All Files" for the STL pack.

- On Printables: click "Download" for STL files.

- On CGTrader: filter by "Free" and select your format (OBJ preferred).

- On GrabCAD: create free account, then download STEP/IGES/STL files.

- Convert between formats using trimesh:
    import trimesh
    mesh = trimesh.load("model.stl")
    mesh.export("model.obj")
    mesh.export("model.glb")
